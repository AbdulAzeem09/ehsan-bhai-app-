Personal Profile (default)

The first profile you get after you register is the "Personal" profile and it is the default profile.

You can have only one personal profile, and you can visit any module with this profile. Personal profile is mostly used as a public profile for friends, so feel free to update your personal profile the way you want your friends to know you!

Limitation: You can not access the Freelancer module with this profile and can not apply to jobs with your Personal Profile. You can not post for a project or job also.

Family Profile (1 Free)

As the name suggests, the Family Profile is here for you so you can now freely connect with all your uncles, aunties, cousins, brothers, sisters and even your parents - just about anyone in your family!

What goes in the family stays in the family! Finally you are free to share anything you want with your family and your friends or business contacts will not have any access to this side of you! :)

Limitation: The family profile will not be able access job module and freelancer module.

Business Profile (2 Free)

Now you can connect with your business associates with confidence through business profiles!

As a business profile you can access all the modules, and are able to post jobs, projects, events, classifed ads, real estate ads. On The SharePage, you can create two business profiles.

Limitation: You can pretty much access all the modules with your business profile. The only limitation is that you can not bid on a project in the Freelancer module with the Business Profile. To bid on a project, you you will need to have a Freelancer profile.

Freelancer Profile (2 Free)

Do you think you can make some extra income in your free time doing freelancing?

Then create a freelancer profile and start earning!b If you are multi-talented then create two different freelancer profiles! And keep your freelancing work completely private from others! Freelancer profile is used in Freelancer module only to bid on projects.

Limitation: You can not do anything on any other module using the Freelancer profile. This profile is only meant to be used in the Freelancer Module. You can have two freelancer profiles with complete different information.

Professional Profile (2 Free)

With a professional profile you can showcase your qualifications, professional achievements and create a professional network by connecting with like-minded professionals.

You can create two professional profiles. You can access all modules and do pretty much everything lwith the Professional Profile with a few exceptions as below:

Limitation: You can not bid on a project or apply for a job, for that you will have to create a Freelancer profile or a an Employment Profile.

Employment Profile (1 Free)

Employment Profile is a private profile, it is not searchable unless you want to make it searchable , and it will only be used within the Job Module. Employment profile will be visible to only those business profiles with whom you share your profile with ie apply to a job. Unless you share your employment profile with another employment profile or business profile, and unless you change the status of your profile to "Public", your employment profile will be kept private and not searchable. You have one employement profile but you can create multiple resume within this profile.
